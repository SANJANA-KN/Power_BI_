# Data Visualization in Power BI

## Introduction
Data visualization is the process of converting data into charts and graphs.

---

# Visualization Types
- Bar Charts  
- Line Charts  
- Pie Charts  
- Tables  
- Cards  

---

# Best Practices
- Keep visuals simple  
- Use correct chart type  
- Avoid clutter  
- Highlight KPIs  

---

# Key Takeaway
Good visualization improves understanding and decision-making.
