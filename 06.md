# Advanced DAX in Power BI

## Introduction
Advanced DAX is used for complex calculations and business logic.

---

# Advanced Functions
- CALCULATE  
- FILTER  
- ALL  
- RELATED  
- TIME INTELLIGENCE  

---

# Example
```DAX
Sales Last Year = CALCULATE(SUM(Sales[Amount]), SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(Date[Date]))
```

---

# Key Takeaway
Advanced DAX enables powerful business insights and time-based analysis.
